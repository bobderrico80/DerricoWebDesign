The Rhythm Randomizer

www.rhythmrandomizer.com

The Rhythm Randomizer is a website I designed while working as a music teacher in a public school. The site generates a random rhythm based on a set of criteria, such as note values, time signature, and phrase length. This site was my first foray into web design.
