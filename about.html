Meet Your Designer

Your site will be personally designed by me, Bob D'Errico. I have experience in creating websites utilizing standard web technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, and PHP. I have an "eye for design," and will be able to build you a site that is rich in both form and function. My detail-oriented approach will be with our every step of the way to ensure the site you want is the site you get.

The Derrico Web Design Process

To begin creating your website, contact me. I will then get in touch with you to discuss your specific needs.

A proof website will then be created for you, which will allow you to check up on the progress of your site as we work together on it.

Then we'll get to the basics: the layout and design. We can either start from scratch, or build off of a similar idea. I can work with as little or as much input as you are willing to give.

Next, we'll focus on the content. I can manage the content for you, or can set it up so that you are in control of updating your site.

Finally, once you are happy with the site, we will be ready to go live. I can guide you through the process of purchasing a domain name if you need one, or if you prefer, you can host your page on our site.

After your site has been created, I will continue to work with you to keep your site up-to-date.
